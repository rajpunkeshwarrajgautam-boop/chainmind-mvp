# Auditor engagement — practical checklist

1. **Select framework** — SOC 2 Type II vs ISO 27001 (often SOC 2 for US SaaS).  
2. **Pick audit firm** — agree timeline, sampling approach, and trust services categories.  
3. **Freeze scope** — product version, environments in scope, excluded legacy systems.  
4. **Evidence room** — shared drive or GRC with indexed artifacts mapped to controls (see `soc2-evidence-matrix.md`).  
5. **Kickoff** — introduce eng leads, show architecture (`infra/k8s`), logging & access paths.  
6. **Fieldwork** — respond to samples (tickets, HR exports, access lists).  
7. **Management letter** — remediate findings; track to closure.  

Legal review of customer-facing policies remains separate from technical controls.
