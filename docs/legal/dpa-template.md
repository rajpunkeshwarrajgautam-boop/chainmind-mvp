# Data Processing Agreement (template — not legal advice)

This document is a **starting point** for counsel to tailor to your entity, customers, and jurisdictions.

1. **Parties** — Data exporter (Customer) and Data importer (ChainMind operator).
2. **Subject matter & duration** — Processing for supply-chain analytics for the subscription term.
3. **Nature & purpose** — Storage, forecasting models, alerting, support access as configured.
4. **Categories of data** — Business operational data (sales, inventory, supplier metadata) provided by Customer.
5. **Subprocessors** — Listed separately; Customer may object per contract terms.
6. **Security measures** — Encryption in transit, access control, audit logging (implement per architecture).
7. **Assistance** — Support for data subject requests as required by applicable law.
8. **Deletion/return** — Upon termination, delete or return data per SOW.

Engage qualified privacy counsel before use.
